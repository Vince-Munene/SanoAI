About Us

SanoAI was conceived from the belief that emotional clarity should be accessible to everyone. Started by a small team of introspective builders, we wanted to offer something quiet yet powerful: a space where anyone could journal freely, feel heard, and begin to understand themselves better—with a little guidance from AI.
